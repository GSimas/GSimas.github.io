Bio

Electronic Engineering undergraduate at Federal University of Santa Catarina (UFSC). FRC 5800 Magic Island Robotics Marketing Mentor. Engineering Intern at Computing and Automation Center of Santa Catarina (CIASC)

Find out more

  • Electronic Engineering UFSC

  • Magic Island Robotics

    FRC 5800 Magic Island Robotics Team. Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts and Mathematics (STEAM) disclosure. FIRST Robotics Competition: 2017 FIRST Steamworks

  • CIASC Internship

    Data Center Monitoring system with Arduino UNO R3 development board. Zabbix System, Data Analysis, 3D Printing

    Noveland Light Novel website for young brazilian authors. Writing and revision; Chryskylodon LN project
